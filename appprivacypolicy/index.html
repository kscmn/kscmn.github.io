Overview

Kato Sports Cards ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Sports Card DB mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information in connection with your use of our App.

Short version: Sports Card DB does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information or user data whatsoever. Everything you enter stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information from users of this App. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • The sports card data you enter into the App
  • Any other data of any kind

How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into Sports Card DB — including card year, set, player name, and card number — is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage (SQLite database). This data never leaves your device.

We have no servers, no cloud storage, and no backend infrastructure. We have no technical ability to access, view, or retrieve any data you enter into the App.

Third-Party Services

Sports Card DB does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. No third-party SDKs that collect data are included in the App.

Internet Access

Sports Card DB does not require or use an internet connection. The App functions entirely offline and makes no network requests.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because we collect no data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since no data is transmitted outside your device, the security of your card collection data is governed by your device's own security features (screen lock, device encryption, etc.). We encourage you to use a secure screen lock on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
